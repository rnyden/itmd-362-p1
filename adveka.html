MOCHIPHON

Our Vision

We strive to introduce a new way to create and play music for physically challenged individuals.

Company Overview

  • We are dedicated to giving physically challenged musicians the ability to play.
  • We wish to introduce music to passionate people previously unable to play.
  • We work to help patients lower stress through music.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate musicians and technically trained students who are eager to apply knowledge and spread the joy of playing an instrument.
